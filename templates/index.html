TROUBLESHOOTING

🍎 1. iPhone: Shaking Does Nothing

Apple blocks motion sensors by default. If shaking does nothing:
1. Open iPhone Settings > Safari.
2. Go to "Privacy & Security".
3. Turn ON "Motion & Orientation Access".
Note: If you clicked "Deny" earlier, go to Phantom Settings > Trusted Apps > Revoke SGOLD Vault, and reconnect.

🌐 2. Wallet Won't Connect

Regular browsers (Chrome/Safari) often block Web3 sensors.
Fix: You must play inside the Phantom App browser. Open Phantom, tap the Globe icon 🌐, and paste our link there.

⏳ 3. Points Not Adding Fast Enough

Shaking violently but only getting points slowly?
Feature, not a bug! We have a 3-second anti-spam cooldown. Shake once, wait for "SUCCESS", then shake again.

👻 4. "Ghost Balance" (Score Reset)

If your score resets after refreshing, the server didn't save it.
Fix: Ensure stable Wi-Fi/5G. If you see "DATABASE ERROR", stop shaking and try later. Always wait for the "SUCCESS!" message before leaving.
